A Project Management Tool

About

Cronus is a project management webapp for project developer teams that is available online. Users are able to plan the project, release their works and track each others’ work on this platform that creates a neat environment for their project management process with the help of Cronus. While users are able to track their process, they can also be on top of things by tracking others’ progress and work. Moreover they can be able to split the work between the teammates and prioritize the tasks. Cronus will bring order for your teamwork projects.
